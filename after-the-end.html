AFTER THE END

ROLE

Concept, Art Director, LEVEL DESIGN


DESCRIPTION

Submitted to Ludum Dare 53 Game Jam The humans have left the Earth leaving a dead planet behind. The few surviving plants and animals are now dying due to high level of pollution. Yet, hope remains. You are one of the last surviving bees of the planet, searching for other members of your tribe, are they alive? For your survival you can pollinate as many flowers as you can so the plants can repopulate and fix the Earth. But be aware! Earth is not what it was before, it's filled with pollutants, acid rain and mutant species. Do not spend too much time in the polluted area, come out for fresh air
