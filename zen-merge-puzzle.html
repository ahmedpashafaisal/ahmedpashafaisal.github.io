ZEN PUZZLE SAGA (PROTOTYPE)

DESCRIPTION

Welcome to Zen Puzzle Saga – the relaxing yet challenging puzzle game! Step into a world of balance, where Yin & Yang orbs move in perfect reflection every time you swipe. Guide these twin orbs through spiked edges, tricky walls, and cunning traps until they unite on the sacred Harmony tile.
