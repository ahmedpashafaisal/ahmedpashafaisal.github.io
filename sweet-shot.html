Sweet Shot

DESCRIPTION

Hyper casual game with puzzle elements made for mobile.
Step into the world of candy making, where you control the flow of delicious candies from the factory pipeline to the jar.
Guide colorful candies through tricky factory pipelines and land them perfectly in the jar. But beware-each level is filled with unique challenges and obstacles that test your strategy and timing!
