SPACE CRICKET

DESCRIPTION

A simple casual game made for mobile.
There is a bat in space, unlike normal cricket bat it’s rotating constantly. However, you can change the direction of rotation by a quick single tap. Timing of your tap is important to swing the bat to hit the target color score.
Each color bar represents a unique cricket score. Be careful of the nasty red color stumps. On hitting those you’ll loose wicket. Smashing boundary enables exciting power-ups and increase your trophies. So swing your bat accordingly and try to hit the target of boundary.
