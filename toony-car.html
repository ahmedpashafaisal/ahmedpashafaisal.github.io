TOONY CAR

DESCRIPTION

An isometric casual car control with lots of cute car to choose from. Each car has unique abilities and unlocking them makes the game even more fun.

YEAR

2017


GENRE

Casual Infinite Runner


PLATFORM

Mobile

CONTRIBUTION

Game Design

The most difficult yet satisfying part of game design for this game was to create the Game Economy system. This game involves a complex economy system that allows the player to buy special cars, fuels and other items to make the game more fun and immersive.

  • Designed the game economy and virtual currency system
  • AI car behavior design
  • Shop Design
  • UI Design

Game Art

This was the first experience of mine to create a 3d low poly environment. As it was intented for mobile, I had to keep the polycount significantly low. But I love the fact that it turned out decent looking.

  • 3D modeling in Blender
  • 6 different Car Design
  • Isometric Environment Design in Low Poly
  • Texturing the assets, optimized for moblie (Baking)
  • Created Visual Effects in Unity
