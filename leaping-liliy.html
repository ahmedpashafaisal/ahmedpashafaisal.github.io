LEAPING LILIY

DESCRIPTION

Leap into a thrilling adventure with *Leaping Liliy Endless Runner*, the ultimate test of your agility, reflexes, and quick thinking!
Dive into a colorful, fast-paced world where every leap counts, and danger lurks at every turn.
Each color bar represents a unique cricket score. Be careful of the nasty red color stumps. On hitting those you’ll loose wicket. Smashing boundary enables exciting power-ups and increase your trophies. So swing your bat accordingly and try to hit the target of boundary.
