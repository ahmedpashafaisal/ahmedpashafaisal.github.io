Summary

I want to craft interactive experiences that take players away into fantastical realms. I'm on a never-ending quest to find fresh, mind-boggling ways to engage and captivate gamers. The game development process is my playground, where collaboration and innovation reign supreme. I want to make games that somehow have a tiny space in the players' memory.
With a strong background in co-founding and managing a small indie game studio, I have gained valuable experience in publishing free-to-play mobile games. My expertise lies in the creation of 2D/3D assets and animation, allowing me to bring captivating visuals and immersive experiences to life.
Driven by a deep love for game design and narration, I am constantly exploring innovative ways to engage players and create memorable gaming experiences. I thrive in the dynamic and collaborative nature of the game development process, and I am always eager to contribute my skills and creativity to bring ideas to fruition.
