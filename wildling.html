Wildling

DESCRIPTION

Blast yourself with a bazooka across one giant rage-filled level in this brutal physics platformer.
Mix and match animal heads and bodies, master precision recoil, and survive deadly traps.
No mercy. No jumps. Just you, a bazooka, and bad decisions.
